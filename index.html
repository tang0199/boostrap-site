Fight The Cold, Ottawa!

Hey, Ottawa, it's cold outside. And not just cold. It's "coldest capital in the world" cold outside.

Who we are

Formed in 2011, Fight The Cold Ottawa (FTCO) is an organization focusing in combating climate change. We cares about how a changing climate affects people, plants and wildlife. We are working together to keep records of the extereme cold weather in Ottawa, spread the awareness about climate changes, and advance solutions.
